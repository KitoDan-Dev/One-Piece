Monkey D' Luffy

Luffy of Straw Hats Pirates, has became known, is the protagonist of the anime, and founder and capitan of Straw Hats Pirates crew. Sice he was very young, his biggest dream is to one day find the lagendary treasure of Gol D. Roger, for become the new King of Pirates.

The first pirate (second member if we count Luffy) to join the Straw Hats Pirates crew, Zoro accepted the Luffy's invitation after the capitan saved his life.

Nami

A war orphan, as a child Nami has adopted by Bell-mere, a Navy woman. While growing up alongside her adoptive sister Nojiko, Nami already demonstrated her passion for drawing maps, dreaming to one day making a map of the entire world.

Usopp

He is a gifted inventor, painter, and sculptor. In combat, he relies primarily on slingshots to fire various kinds of ammunition with great precision in coordination with a set of lies and other weapons giving him a unique fighting style named "The Usopp Arsenal".

This little reindeer gained the hability to change its shape and think like a humans after eat the Hito Hito no Mi Fruit. Chopper is a valued member of the Straw Hats Pirates crew, serving as the crew's doctor.
